### Effective Proof Strategies

1. **Mathlib API wrappers** (most common): Direct use of existing Mathlib declarations via `exact`, `simp`, `apply`. Worked for ~14 items.
2. **Instance resolution**: Lean's typeclass system handles many definition-style items automatically once the right instance is stated.
3. **Tactic proofs with `ring`/`nlinarith`**: Effective for algebraic computations (Ex 1.29 minimal polynomial).
4. **Contradiction/by_contra**: Used for non-membership proofs (Ex 1.24 Z[√5], Ex 1.29 non-integrality).
5. **Theorem wrapping**: Stating theorems as thin wrappers around Mathlib's existing theorems (Thm 1.8 Ostrowski, Thm 1.16, Prop 1.28).
